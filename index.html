How did we identify the problem?

Everyone struggles with finding the right gift for someone the person. The idea came to us on the occasion of a friend's birthday and it required a lot of thinking time and the time could be shortened if we had a trial automated that thought in advance the idea for the perfect gift.

What you thought is a solution for the problem?

Developing a virtual system that decides what gift would suit them best based on user data about that person.

Your plan for customer discovery

This idea is addressed to the public who are short of ideas, are not very creative and want something for the last hundred meters.

Your plan for customer discovery (detailed)

We opted for an online questionnaire. It contains the following questions.

  • Do you consider yourself a creative person when it comes to finding the right gift?/ Do you come up with ideas for finding the perfect gifts quickly?

  • Have you always been satisfied with the choice of gift by your dear friends?

  • Do you think that the people you gave gifts to were satisfied with your choice?

  • How much effort did you put into finding the right gift?

  • Do you prefer personalized gifts or bought from stores directly as they are?

  • Do you prefer to buy all the gifts in one place or to search in several places? (for collaboration with other stores)

  • What was the most frequently purchased gift so far, if there is one?

Describe how these insights have affected your product (if in any way)?

Initially we did not want to introduce the Machine Learning part for MVP, but we received some suggestions of already implemented libraries that would be useful to us. Having the international product market at our disposal, we thought of letting users describe their needs in any language for precision and ease of expression.

Do you consider that you are ready to move to the Customer Validation stage (to test selling)? Provide arguments.

Yes, because the prototype can be built easily. It will integrate certain libraries that train various models for Machine Learning algorithms and thus, we can automate with the help of artificial intelligence that identifies gifts based on text.

Our Market Strategy

We asked random people about our concept and most interested were men. They considered a great idea for an application, because it would save time finding the perfect gift, especially for their lovers or friends. Following this case study, we decided that finding gifts for couples is the niche that we want to activate in.

Market Target Size

Personalized Gifts Sub-Market

For this topic, we analyzed a related sub-market which is called personalized gifts from 2018 to 2019, with a forecast for 2020 and 2026 expressed in million U.S. dollars. This was somehow related to guide us into the gifting industry workaround.

According to estimates and forecasts, the global personalized gifts market is set to increase considerably over the coming years: in 2019, the worldwide personalized gifts market was valued at about 23.5 billion U.S. dollars. By 2026, the market size is projected to grow to an estimated 34.3 billion U.S. dollars. Research article is from Statista.

Gift Retailers Sub-Market

In order to explore another sub-market, we also have the possibility to become retailers to increase our profit margin.

The gifts retailing market is estimated to grow at a CAGR of 3.01% between 2022 and 2027. The size of the market is forecast to increase by USD 13,491.69 million. The growth of the market depends on serval factors, including the rise in demand for seasonal decorations and a growing gifting culture, the growing popularity of private-label brands and expansion in distribution channels and social media marketing. This report extensively covers market segmentation by distribution channel (offline and online), product (souvenirs and novelty items, seasonal decorations, greeting cards, giftware, and other gift items), and geography (Europe, North America, APAC, South America, and Middle East and Africa). It also includes an in-depth analysis of drivers, trends, and challenges. Furthermore, the report includes historic market data from 2017 to 2021. Inspiration source is from this Technavio article.

Players & Potential Competitors

Our target market is not defined exactly due to the fact that we want to solve a problem that is not implemented yet, therefore this fact will create a new "market". Possible competitors might be the greatest retailers for objects in general, such as Macy's, JCPenney, Sears Holding, and House of Fraser. These 4 competitors offer a variety of gift products through their gift retailing stores. The presence of numerous players in the market increases the market competition, which in turn, can make people use the already known shops for finding gifts. As for affiliate marketing, we encounter the problem that there are globally known retailers such as Amazon Marketplace, Ebay etc. which can be challenging to get in touch with. To determine an aproximate range number, the list can be infinite, but over 6 competitors adding from all the sub-markets.

Macy's

Macy's is a chain of stores which are dedicated for all kind of decorations and gifts in general. Its market share for 2022 Q3 is about 1.96% according Google statistics.

JCPenney

As Macy's, JCPenney targets the same market and uses the same concept but recently it has suffered a downgrade in its sales and income. Its market share for 2020 Q3 is about 0.55% according Google statistics which is way lower than Macy's.

Amazon Marketplace

To include a company from affiliate marketing, we chosen Amazon as it is the leader of the market. According to Wikipedia, Amazon Marketplace is an e-commerce platform owned and operated by Amazon that enables third-party sellers to sell new or used products on a fixed-price online marketplace alongside Amazon's regular offerings. Using Amazon Marketplace, third-party sellers gain access to Amazon's customer base, and Amazon expands the offerings on its site without having to invest in additional inventory. In 2021, its market share was up to 50% which is enormous and demonstrates that is very used and much trusted from the users.
